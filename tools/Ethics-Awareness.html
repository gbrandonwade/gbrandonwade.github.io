AI Ethics Awareness & Education

This educational tool helps teams and individuals understand AI ethics concepts and identify areas where expert guidance is needed. It's designed for learning and discussion, not as a comprehensive implementation guide.

Learning Focus

Understand key ethical considerations across AI project lifecycles. Identify blind spots and areas needing deeper expertise.

Discussion Starter

Generate meaningful conversations about ethics within your team or organization. Surface important questions early.

Gap Identification

Discover what you don't know and where you need expert consultation, specialized tools, or additional training.

Knowledge Testing

Assess your understanding of AI ethics concepts through interactive scenarios and questions.

Resource Discovery

Find authoritative sources for deeper learning and professional guidance on ethical AI implementation.

Awareness Report

Generate a summary of your current understanding and recommended next steps for learning.

What This Tool Does NOT Do

  • Replace Expert Consultation: Complex ethical decisions require domain experts and ethicists
  • Ensure Compliance: Legal and regulatory compliance needs professional legal review
  • Provide Implementation Details: Specific technical implementation requires specialized knowledge
  • Guarantee Ethical Outcomes: Ethics is an ongoing practice, not a one-time checklist
